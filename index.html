- simple visual interface for composing short melodies
- vast number of permutations available by sequencing 12 note melodies.
- released: 2020

My Tracks

Analog Tom - a 4 minute electronica track. Inspired by EDM/IDM, Analog Tom was created entirely with a drum sample pack and freeware synths.

Instruments (Software): Sitala VST sampler, Digits VST synth, Ardour DAW.

RTSEQS128 - a 3 minute track produced in Italy. RTSEQS128 was coded in PureData and SonicPi (SuperCollider) and then mixed with Ardour. The piece features a set of chords that have a fluctuating pulse (coded in PureData), in a kind of wave pattern, and layered over this there is a melody and a more percussive track (coded in SonicPi) that slides between perceptible pulse and a tone.

Instruments (Software): PureData, SonicPi, Digits VST synth, Ardour DAW.
